Joanne Choi

Asian cuisines as well as healthy food and nutrition are something that Joanne knows a lot about. Joanne has been in the culinary profession for almost 20 years. Her previous work at Solaire Resort and Hotel developed her skills in preparing special food (based on different diets), wellness and balanced meals, as well as individual preferences. She has also done a lot of consulting jobs for Internationl Nando Hotel Consultancy Incorporated. Her culinary career also took her to South America and Toronto, Canada to work for a well-known Korean international restaurant chain which specializes on the standards of fast food business and franchising. At the moment, she is handling our Fundamentals of Culinary Arts short course, Asian cuisine courses, and courses focused on healthy food.

Mats Rosenberg

Having begun his career at the tender age of 20 in his native Australian, Mats entered another stage in his life when he founded Saing Culinary Academy with his partners. As an instructor, he is recognized for his strong grasp of culinary fundamentals and global standards, a result of honing his skills in several Michelin star restaurants around the world such as the Villa Källhagen in Stockholm, Westra Piren in Gothenburg, Sweden and Le Meridien in Piccadilly, London. At the Restaurant Akademin in Sweden, he worked with the academic head to offer further educational courses for restaurant professionals. He then mastered the nuances of mass production, purchasing, food chemistry and international flavors working as an Executive Chef in several cruise ships for 10 years, including the MS Endeavour and MS Polaris. He shares his knowledge and passion with future culinary artists through his Saing Culinary Academy classes.

Grace Maxx

Grace Maxx has been a baker and an artist by heart since her grade school years. Growing up, she has excelled and pursued her passion for both crafts – first by finishing college with a degree in Fine Arts and later by getting her diploma in Pastry Arts from the Institute of Culinary Education in New York City. Her combined passion for both arts opened a lot of opportunities for her to enhance her expertise at famous restaurants and patisserie shops such as Financier Patisserie, David Burke at Bloomingdales and the world-class restaurant Nobu, all located in the Big Apple.
